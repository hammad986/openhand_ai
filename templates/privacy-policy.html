1. Overview

Nexora AI ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data when you use the Nexora AI platform at nexora.ai ("Service").

By using our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of data:

  • Account data: Your name, email address, and hashed password when you register.
  • Usage data: Tasks submitted, AI outputs generated, token consumption, session timestamps, and feature interactions.
  • AI inputs: Prompts, code, and instructions you submit to the AI agents. These are used solely to generate responses and for improving the platform.
  • Payment data: Subscription tier and payment confirmation identifiers. We do not store full card numbers — these are handled by our payment processor (Razorpay).
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information, and cookies for session management and security.
  • Support data: Messages you send through our support system.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the Nexora AI platform.
  • Authenticate your identity and manage your session securely.
  • Process subscription payments and send payment receipts.
  • Send essential account emails (verification, password reset, billing notices).
  • Respond to support requests and troubleshoot issues.
  • Monitor platform security, detect abuse, and enforce our Terms of Service.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

We work with the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • AI Providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini, Groq, etc.) — Your prompts are sent to these providers to generate AI responses. Each provider has their own data retention and privacy policies.
  • Razorpay — Payment processing. Razorpay handles card data under PCI-DSS compliance and their own privacy policy.
  • Resend — Transactional email delivery (verification links, password resets, invoices).
  • Replit — Hosting and infrastructure provider.

We share only the minimum data necessary with each provider for them to perform their service.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Specifically:

  • Account data is retained until you request deletion.
  • Session & usage data is kept for 90 days for security and analytics purposes.
  • AI interaction logs are stored for 30 days unless you explicitly save them as sessions.
  • Payment records are retained for 7 years for legal/tax compliance.
  • Password reset tokens expire in 30 minutes and are purged after use.
  • Email verification tokens expire in 24 hours.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Passwords hashed with bcrypt (cost factor 12)
  • JWT access tokens with short 15-minute expiry
  • Refresh token rotation with per-device session tracking
  • SHA-256 hashed reset/verification tokens (raw token never stored)
  • HTTPS encryption in transit
  • Rate limiting on all sensitive endpoints

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies to maintain your login session. We also use localStorage to store your access and refresh tokens client-side. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. A cookie notice is displayed on your first visit.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@nexora.ai or use the data request option in your account settings.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed at anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with their data, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice on our platform or sending an email to your registered address. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the latest revision.
