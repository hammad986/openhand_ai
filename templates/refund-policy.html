Refund Policy
Last updated: May 2, 2026  ·  We want you to be satisfied with your subscription.

1. Subscription Model

Nexora AI operates on a monthly recurring subscription basis. When you subscribe to a paid plan, you are billed for the full billing period (one month) in advance. Subscriptions automatically renew at the end of each period unless cancelled.

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your Account Settings or by contacting support. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period — you retain access to paid features until then.

2. Refund Eligibility

ELIGIBLE FOR REFUND
  • Charged after cancellation request
  • Duplicate charges for the same period
  • Service unavailable >72 hours in a billing period
  • Technical error causing incorrect charge
  • First-time subscriber within 7 days of first payment
NOT ELIGIBLE FOR REFUND
  • Change of mind after using the Service
  • Partial month usage after cancellation
  • Account suspended for Terms violations
  • Unused AI credits or token allowance
  • Third-party API costs you incurred (BYOK)

3. 7-Day Satisfaction Guarantee

If you are a first-time paid subscriber and are not satisfied with the Service, you may request a full refund within 7 days of your first payment. This guarantee applies only once per customer account and requires that you have not violated our Terms of Service.

4. Service Downtime Credits

If the Nexora AI platform experiences verified downtime exceeding 72 cumulative hours within a single billing month, eligible subscribers may request a pro-rated credit applied to the next billing period. Scheduled maintenance windows announced 48 hours in advance do not count toward downtime.

5. Non-Refundable Items

The following are explicitly non-refundable:

  • Any partial-period usage after voluntary downgrade or cancellation.
  • Token usage consumed during the billing period.
  • API costs for BYOK (Bring Your Own Key) configurations — these are billed by the respective AI provider.
  • Accounts terminated due to violations of our Terms of Service.
  • Add-on features, one-time purchases, or promotional pricing items.

6. How to Request a Refund

1

Email Support

Send an email to support@nexora.ai with the subject line "Refund Request".

2

Include Your Details

Provide your registered email address, the approximate date of the charge, and the reason for the request.

3

Review Period

We will review your request within 3–5 business days and notify you of our decision by email.

4

Refund Processing

Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method within 5–10 business days, depending on your bank or card issuer.

7. Chargebacks

We strongly encourage you to contact us before initiating a chargeback with your bank. Fraudulent chargebacks may result in account suspension. We are committed to resolving legitimate disputes fairly and promptly.

8. Cancellation vs. Refund

Cancelling your subscription stops future billing but does not automatically entitle you to a refund for the current period. If you believe you qualify for a refund, you must request one explicitly using the process above.
